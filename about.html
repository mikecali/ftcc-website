About FTCC

A community built
on Bayanihan

We are a duly-incorporated association in the Australian Capital Territory, founded to connect and empower Filipino ICT professionals in Canberra.

Our purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To represent Filipino professionals as highly-skilled and globally competitive talents contributing to Australia's goal of becoming a top digital economy by 2030.

🎯 Our Mission

To connect, support, and upskill Filipino ICT professionals in Australia, providing a warm and nurturing space where each member can grow and thrive as both an individual and collaborator.

Our story

How FTCC began

FTCC started from a simple but powerful idea: give back to the community. Co-founder Arvi Vito found others who shared the same passion, to help others and create a genuine support network for ICT professionals in Australia.

What began as a gathering of like-minded individuals has grown into a formally incorporated association in the ACT, with a structured programs offering real career support, mentorship, networking, and community for Filipino tech professionals across Canberra.

FTCC has been recognised by the Philippine Embassy in Canberra as part of Australia's engagement with the Filipino STEM community, an acknowledgement of the real impact FTCC has on strengthening bilateral cooperation on science, technology and innovation.
