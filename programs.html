What we offer

Programs & services
for the community

From career development to hackathons, everything FTCC offers is built around helping Filipino tech professionals in Canberra grow, connect and lead.

Professional development

Programs designed to accelerate careers and build the next generation of Filipino ICT leaders.

📈
